Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on 28-03-2025 19:59:41

At Jet Yatra, we are committed to providing a smooth and pleasant travel experience for all of our customers. In case you need to cancel or modify your travel plans, we have established the following Cancellation and Refund Policy to ensure fairness and clarity for all parties involved.

Cancellation Policy:

We understand that plans can change unexpectedly. Therefore, we allow cancellations under the following conditions:

  • Cancellations will only be accepted if made within 24 hours of booking, provided the travel service has not yet been processed or confirmed with service providers (hotels, airlines, tours, etc.).
  • For bookings that have already been confirmed and processed with our vendors, cancellations may incur charges based on the vendor’s policy.
  • Cancellation requests must be made in writing, either through our customer support email or website contact form.
  • Special packages, such as holiday packages or custom tours, may have different cancellation terms, which will be communicated at the time of booking.

Refund Policy:

In case you decide to cancel your booking, the following refund conditions will apply:

  • If you cancel your booking within the cancellation period (24 hours after booking), you will be entitled to a full refund, minus any processing fees.
  • If the cancellation request is made after the initial cancellation period, we will process a partial refund, subject to deductions from our service providers. The refund amount will depend on the cancellation policies of the airlines, hotels, and other service providers.
  • For travel services such as flights or train bookings, which may have non-refundable tickets, you will only be refunded the amount recoverable from the third-party vendors.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the cancellation is confirmed and once all third-party vendor refund processes are complete.

Damaged or Defective Services:

If you experience issues with your travel services (such as damaged or unsatisfactory services), please contact our customer service team within 24 hours of experiencing the issue. We will work with our service providers to address the concern and process either a replacement or refund depending on the situation.


Force Majeure:

In case of natural disasters, strikes, or any other force majeure event that causes travel disruption, we may not be able to process cancellations or refunds. In such cases, we will work with our service providers to offer alternative options or reschedule your travel plans.


Refund Processing Time:

Refunds, if applicable, will be processed to the original payment method. The exact timeline may vary depending on your payment provider but can take anywhere from 7 to 10 business days. Please contact us if you experience any delays beyond this period.

If you have any questions or concerns about our cancellation and refund policy, please do not hesitate to reach out to our customer support team. We are here to assist you and ensure a smooth travel experience.
